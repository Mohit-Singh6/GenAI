## Chunking:
- Chunking is the process of breaking down large documents into smaller, manageable pieces (chunks) for easier processing and analysis.
- It's not a small or easy thing to do, and it requires careful consideration of the document's structure, content, and the specific use case.
- There are multiple ways to chunk a document, and the choice of method can significantly impact the quality of the results. The choice depends on the document's structure, content, and the specific use case. 


## Terms:
- RAG Fusion
- Reciprocal Rank Fusion
- Corrective RAG
- Adaptive RAG
- Agentic RAG

We use graphDB too (not just the vector db) to store the relationships between the chunks and the original document. This allows us to retrieve the relevant chunks based on their relationships, rather than just their content.
